Spaghetti

Photo by Dana Tentis

Description

This house spagetti recipe aims to keep things simple ,but delicious. While making your own marinara sauce from scratch may seem daunting at first, it can be accomplished fairly quickly and with only a few quality, fresh ingredients.

Once you feel comfortable making this recipe, I encourage you to make it your own by making modifications and adding personal touches to suit your tastes. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • San Marzano tomatoes (canned, whole)
  • Garlic, 1 head
  • Yellow Onion, 1 large
  • Mushrooms, washed and sliced
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil, 1/4 cup, divided
  • Dry spaghetti noodles, 1 lb.
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black Pepper, freshly cracked, to taste
  • Red Pepper Flakes, to taste
  • Parmesan Cheese, for serving
  • Fresh Basil, optional, for serving

Steps

  1. Peel and mince garlic, peel and small-dice onion, wash and slice mushrooms
  2. Once all vegetables are prepped, set a dutch oven or large saucepan to medium heat and add 1 Tbsp of olive oil
  3. When oil begins to shimmer, add diced onions and lightly salt/pepper
  4. Cook onions, stirring occasionally, for 3-5 minutes, or until slightly softened and translucent
  5. Clear a space in the center of your pot and add minced garlic, stirring constantly for 30-60 seconds or until fragrant -- be careful to not let garlic burn
  6. Transfer onions and garlic to a separate dish
  7. Add 1 Tbsp to pot
  8. Once shimmering, add sliced mushrooms with salt and pepper, stirring occasionally for 5-10 minutes, or until softened and lightly browned
  9. Add remaining olive oil to pot
  10. Return onions and garlic to pot, stirring for 30-60 seconds
  11. Add canned tomatoes to pot, using spoon to break up slightly
  12. Reduce heat to low and let simmer for 30 minutes, or until flavors are developed and sauce has reduced slightly
  13. Season to taste with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes and keep on low heat
  14. Bring large pot of very salted water to boil
  15. Once boiling, add dry spaghetti noodles and cook according to package instructions, or until al dente
  16. Once pasta is cooked, reserve 1/2 cup of its cooking water than drain
  17. Add drained pasta to sauce pot with half of your pasta cooking water, stirring frequently until all pasta is well-coated, slowly adding more cooking water as needed
  18. Remove from heat and serve in bowls
  19. Top with parmesan and basil, as desired
